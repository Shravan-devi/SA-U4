Cultural Textile Crafts

By-Shravan Devi

Textiles as the name suggests refers to the latin word "textere" meaning to be woven crafts. In India there are a variety of such Textile crafts and patterns produced ranging from the cultures of Rajasthan, Gujrat, Andhra Pradesh, West Bengal, etc. Every place has their own uniqueness in terms of their own textile crafts.
